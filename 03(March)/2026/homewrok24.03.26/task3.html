Tag

[tæg]

noun

  1. a label attached to someone or something for the purpose of identification.

    "he gave his pet a collar with a metal name tag"

    synonyms: label, ticket, badge, mark, marker, tab

  2. a small piece or part that is attached to a main body.

verb

  1. attach a label to.

    "mothers suspected that their babies had been wrongly tagged"

    synonyms: label, attach tags to, put a label on, mark

  2. add to something, especially as an afterthought.

    "she meant to tag her question on at the end of her remarks"

    synonyms: add, tack, join, attach, append
